Welcome to the Jinx Website!

Jinx is a cat that rose to fame on the internet via social media platforms TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter. Jinx, also known as Jinxie or Big Foot Jinx, is known for her prominent eyes and feet, comically larger than the typical house cat. The feet of Jinx is relatively long, comparable to clown shoes or, as the moniker suggests, the cryptic folklore creature known as Bigfoot. The eyes of Jinx are wide open and noticeably larger at a first glance, peering at you in such a way that it is adored by millions of people online. Jinx, and her owner Mia, gained a lot of popularity in 2021, and it can be accredited that social platforms helped bring forth this amazing and beautiful cat.
